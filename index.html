Gunsmith Range

A 3D FPS campaign built in pure Three.js — 10 missions, a weapon system with attachments and AI enemies.

Three.js Vanilla JS · ES Modules WebGL Web Audio API
  • 10 campaign missions across unique locations (warehouse, outpost, street, house, industrial site, bunker, airport, train, port, complex)
  • 8 weapons (rifle, pistol, SMG, DMR, shotgun, LMG + 2 purchasable: OSA bullpup, GRZMOT revolver) and ~14 attachments (sights, magazines, grips, suppressors)
  • Three AI enemy types (shooter, tactical crouch, knifeman) with procedural animation
  • Wave system with captain narration between stages
  • Economy (credits), weapon unlocks and progress saved in localStorage
  • Grenades (frag + flashbang) with flight physics
  • Touch controls on mobile (virtual joystick)
  • Optimized rendering (light culling, raycast filtering, GPU tuning)

TubeStar: Youtuber Simulator

A 3D life sim of an online creator — explore your room, record and edit videos, grow your channel and climb a leaderboard against a hundred NPCs.

Three.js Vite Vanilla JS · ES Modules localStorage
  • Explorable 3D room with upgrades across 3 apartment tiers (with a move-out cutscene)
  • Record & edit content in 5 minigame genres (arcade, racing, shooter, puzzle, horror) + music and livestreaming
  • Full YouTube economy: gear, CPM payouts, trends, content-variety bonus
  • Top 100 leaderboard of 100 dynamically-growing NPC creators
  • 50 achievements across 6 rarity tiers with a notification system
  • Wardrobe of 11 unlockable character looks
  • 10 pets wandering around the room
  • In-game "internet" with 6 themed shops (food, gear, furniture, games, pets, real estate)
  • Fan events with a hype minigame and milestone email notifications

🛠️ Want to test something specific? Press ` (backtick) to open the dev panel — it lets you edit any game number (cash, subs, etc.) live, so you can jump straight to any feature:

  • Buy/equip a skin: open the dev panel, bump your cash or subscriber count, close it, then click the wardrobe/closet in your room to open the Wardrobe and buy or equip any look.
  • Buy an apartment: open the dev panel, set cash to at least $10,000 (Tier 2) or $100,000 (Tier 3), close it, then open the desktop's Internet app → KeyList Realty → Buy → Move in.
  • Go to a fan event: open the dev panel, set subscribers to 1,000+, close it, then open the Gmail app on the desktop — you'll have an event invite email, click "Accept & Go to Event".
