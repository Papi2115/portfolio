TubeStar: Youtuber Simulator

A 3D life sim of an online creator — explore your room, record and edit videos, grow your channel and climb a leaderboard against a hundred NPCs. Move into bigger apartments, adopt pets, dress up your character and get invited to fan events as you blow up. Playable on desktop and mobile, mouse/touch only.

Three.js Vite Vanilla JS · ES Modules localStorage Mobile-friendly

What's in it

  • Explorable 3D room, upgradable across 3 apartment tiers with a move-in cutscene
  • Record & edit content across 5 minigame genres (arcade, racing, shooter, puzzle, horror), each with 3 distinct price tiers, plus music and livestreaming
  • Full YouTube economy: gear upgrades, CPM payouts, weekly trends, content-variety bonus
  • Top 100 leaderboard of dynamically-growing NPC creators
  • 50 achievements across 6 rarity tiers, each paying out cash, with a toast notification system
  • Wardrobe: 10 unlockable character skins plus a full accessories tab (hats, glasses, rare mythic recolors), gated by cash or subscriber milestones
  • 10 collectible pets wandering independently around the room
  • In-game "internet" with 6 themed shops (food, gear, furniture, games, pets, real estate)
  • Fan events with a hype minigame and milestone email invites
  • Always-on rotating goals panel nudging you toward the next thing to try, each worth a small cash bonus
  • Non-blocking crisis system: a banner warns you and counts down instead of a hard stop
  • Full sound design (music + sound effects) and touch controls on phones/tablets, portrait or landscape

🛠️ Want to test something specific? Press ` (backtick) to open the dev panel — it lets you edit any game number (cash, subs, etc.) live, so you can jump straight to any feature:

  • Buy/equip a skin or accessory: open the dev panel, bump your cash or subscriber count, close it, then click the wardrobe/closet in your room to open the Wardrobe and buy or equip any look.
  • Buy an apartment: open the dev panel, set cash to at least $10,000 (Tier 2) or $100,000 (Tier 3), close it, then open the desktop's Internet app → KeyList Realty → Buy → Move in.
  • Adopt a pet: open the dev panel, bump your cash, close it, then open the desktop's Internet app → PetPals → Adopt.
  • Go to a fan event: open the dev panel, set subscribers to 1,000+, close it, then open the Gmail app on the desktop — you'll have an event invite email, click "Accept & Go to Event".
